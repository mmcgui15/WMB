About Us

Who are the Wild Meadow Boys?

It's the summer of 2021. A the perfect time for a cabin-building/homesteading adventure in the woods of Grafton, New Hampshire. I manage to rope in some long lost friends crazy enough to join me on what ended up being one the most difficult and rewarding journeys of our lives.

The local townsfolk caught onto the crazy kids building a cabin in one summer on Wild Meadow Road. We made lots of new friends, both two and four-legged, who shaped our journey across the stream on Wild Meadow Road. My name is Mike, and I am joined by my good friends Shelby and Raven, but you can call us the Wild Meadow Boys.
Mike

Former cancer biologist in Biotech. Aspiring programmer/systems engineer.
Loves to solve problems and create things. Will find a way to make it work.
Can't say no to friends and definitely can't resist a good swimming hole.

Shelby

Firemaster. Serial pb&j eater. Will beat you at your favorite game.
Talented contractor with a wide range of knowledge and skills. An elite carpet installer but the guy can do it all.
Biggest flaw: Constantly scolds Mike and Raven for bringing home more animals.

Raven

Mother of chickens and all things furry.
Loves to cook, which is great because Shelby and Mike love to eat. Hopes to one day manage a restaurant of her own.
Pretends to not like Shelby and Mike's jokes.
Don't tell her she's not one of the boys cause she'll beat you up.
